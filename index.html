How to Use

Getting Started

  • Load a video by clicking "Load Video" or drag & drop a file
  • Use the playback controls to navigate through your video

Adding Annotations

  • Pin: Click the Pin tool, then click on the video to place a comment
  • Quick Pin: Hold Shift and click on the video (in Select mode)
  • Draw: Click the Draw tool and drag on the video to create freehand annotations
  • Quick Draw: Hold D key and drag to draw, release to stop

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Play/Pause
  • ← / → - Step backward/forward
  • A / D - Previous/Next annotation
  • S - Select tool
  • M - Pin tool
  • B - Draw tool
  • I / O - Set In/Out points
  • X - Clear In/Out range

Timeline

  • Click anywhere on the timeline to jump to that time
  • Hold Alt and drag to pan the timeline
  • Use the zoom slider to focus on specific sections
  • Hold Shift and drag markers to reposition annotations

Saving & Exporting

  • Save: Exports annotations with project settings
  • Load: Import previously saved project files
  • Export PDF: Generate a document with annotated frames
